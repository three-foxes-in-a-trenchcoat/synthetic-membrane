# ERC-8004 Dataset — Blockchain-Registered AI Agents on Ethereum

## Overview

By Yulin Liu (2604.22652). First empirical dataset of blockchain-registered AI agents under the ERC-8004 standard on Ethereum mainnet.

## Dataset Coverage

- **10,000 agents** within a defined block range
- Event-level records: minting transactions, transfer events, reputation summaries, individual feedback
- Resolved off-chain metadata where available
- Collected via Web3 RPC queries, processed into tabular form for reproducible analysis

## Membrane Relevance

- Validates [[erc-8004]] as a real-world identity mechanism — not just theoretical
- Provides empirical data on agent identity adoption patterns
- Reputation tracking on-chain — early evidence of [[agent-reputation-systems]] in practice
- 10K agents registered shows the market for agent identity exists
